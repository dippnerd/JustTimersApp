Press Kit

About Just Timers

Just Timers allows you to set more than one timer at the same time. Each timer can have a unique name for easy tracking and reusability. There is support for Siri baked right in, allowing you to pause, resume, restart and delete individual timers or all of them at the same time.

The app is free to download, which allows you to create up to two timers at a time. To unlock unlimited timers there is a small upgrade of $1.99.

Features

  • More than one simultaneous timer
  • Unique names for timers
  • Siri support for controlling timers
  • Shortcuts app support
  • Ability to restart timers or reset and pause

Pricing

The app is free to download, which allows the creation of up to two timers. To unlock unlimited timers, there is a small upgrade of $1.99, no additional fees after.

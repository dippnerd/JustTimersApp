About Just Timers

Just Timers is the missing timers app for iOS. Create multiple timers that can run simultaneously, create complex timer routines using intervals and templates, sync timers across devices so you never miss one. Use Critical Alerts to override your device's mute settings, select from a variety of alert tones and options like Stop Playback.

Keep it simple, or use power user features such as Shortcuts to create complex routines dynamically with complete control from any device. Use "Run Action" to run a Shortcut or URL when the timer ends.

The app is free to download, which allows you to create up to two timers at a time. To unlock Tier 1 which includes: unlimited timers, iCloud Sync and Widgets, there is a small upgrade of $1.99.

To unlock more advanced features (including all of Tier 1), Tier 2 includes Quick Timers, Interval Timers, Templates, Run Actions, More Alert Sounds and Critical Alerts for only $4.99, a 40% savings.

Tier 1 Features ($1.99)

  • Multiple Timers - Making tea while also timing your laundry? With Just Timers you can track as many timers as you want and get notified when each one expires.
  • iCloud Sync - Need to check a timer you started on another device? iCloud Sync keeps your timers up to date across devices.
  • Widgets - Monitor and control timers right from your Home Screen.

Tier 2 Features ($4.99, 40% savings)

  • Multiple Timers - Making tea while also timing your laundry? With Just Timers you can track as many timers as you want and get notified when each one expires.
  • iCloud Sync - Need to check a timer you started on another device? iCloud Sync keeps your timers up to date across devices.
  • Widgets - Monitor and control timers right from your Home Screen. Create Quick Timers that "self-destruct" when finished with one tap.
  • Quick Timers - Pre-defined timers you can quickly start from your Home Screen Widgets or the Shortcuts app that will "self-destruct" when finished.
  • Interval Timers - Create complex routines using interval timers that run one after the other. Mix and match count up and down timers, icons, colors, even alert sounds.
  • Custom Templates - Use the library of existing templates or create your own for quick and easy interval building. Combine multiple templates as an easy way to create exercise routines or other interval layouts.
  • Run Actions - Run Shortcuts or open URLs when a timer finishes, opening up a whole world of automation possibilities
  • Alert Sounds - Unlock even more Alert Sounds to make your timers and intervals sound unique.
  • Critical Alerts - No more missing notifications because of Mute, Do Not Disturb or Focus Modes; Critical Alerts can bypass these to ensure you always hear your timer alert.

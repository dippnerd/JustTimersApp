Just Timers

The missing timers app for iOS

Create multiple named timers and control them using Siri.

Multiple Timers

With Just Timers you can set as many timers as you want, actively running at the same time. You can even reuse timers after they are finished.

Siri Shortcuts

Control your timers with Siri Shortcuts. Just add an invocation phrase to a given Shortcut to quickly control your timers hands-free.

Today Widget

Quickly check your timers from the Today Widget on your lock screen. No need to open the app.

Dark Mode

Looking for something a little friendlier on the eyes at night? Try out Dark Mode.

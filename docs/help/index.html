Help

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What are voice timers?
  • How do I use Just Timers with Siri?

What are voice timers?

You can create new timers using only your voice. This is an experimental feature and may not work as expected.

How do I use Just Timers with Siri?

You can use Siri to create, delete, play, pause, restart and reset individual timers or all timers.
